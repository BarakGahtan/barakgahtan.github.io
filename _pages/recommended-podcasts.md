A selection of running podcasts that I listen to regularly. From training advice to inspiring interviews, these are great companions for easy runs and long drives.

---

## Top Picks

### The Rich Roll Podcast
**Host:** Rich Roll &middot; **Frequency:** Twice weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 120 min

Ultra-endurance athlete Rich Roll hosts deep-dive conversations with thought leaders, athletes, and change-makers. Episodes often run 2+ hours and explore the intersection of athletics, wellness, and purpose.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-rich-roll-podcast/id582272991) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/4hpASXqbC0K30zxPqPKePi) &middot; [Website](https://www.richroll.com/all-episodes/)

---

### The Morning Shakeout
**Host:** Mario Fraioli &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 50 min

Thoughtful conversations with runners, coaches, and thinkers who shape the sport. Mario's interviewing style brings out unique insights and stories from his guests.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-morning-shakeout/id1070723888) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/4ybWO8IUPO4v3e3G2L6D0g) &middot; [Website](https://themorningshakeout.com/podcast/)

---

### Marathon Training Academy
**Host:** Angie & Trevor Spencer &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 45 min

Practical marathon training advice for everyday runners from coaches who understand the realities of training with jobs, families, and limited time. Great for first-time marathoners through experienced racers.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/marathon-training-academy/id506748958) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/4eDU2J6s6sShPqPWYJCQzF) &middot; [Website](https://marathontrainingacademy.com/)

---

### The Strength Running Podcast
**Host:** Jason Fitzgerald &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 40 min

USA Track & Field certified coach Jason Fitzgerald shares evidence-based training advice, interviews with elite runners and coaches, and strategies for running faster while staying healthy.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-strength-running-podcast/id826107725) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/2RJx2EEqoeTvfWbwsEuSP9) &middot; [Website](https://strengthrunning.com/podcast/)

## Training & Science

### Run to the Top
**Host:** Runners Connect &middot; **Frequency:** Twice weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 35 min

Training-focused podcast from the coaching team at Runners Connect. Evidence-based discussions on training, nutrition, and injury prevention.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/run-to-the-top-podcast/id901182622) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/4aXs2q5dPcYVVqCAffBNxj) &middot; [Website](https://runnersconnect.net/running-podcast/)

### KoopCast
**Host:** Jason Koop &middot; **Frequency:** Bi-weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 60 min

Ultrarunning coach Jason Koop dives deep into the science and practice of ultra and trail running. Technical content for serious trail runners seeking to understand the why behind training.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/koopcast/id1438879086) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/0Zz97O64nRqKGH0oLlPm5E) &middot; [Website](https://trainright.com/koopcast/)

### Science of Ultra
**Host:** Shawn Bearden, PhD &middot; **Frequency:** Monthly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 75 min

Deep dives into the scientific literature relevant to ultrarunning and endurance sports. Perfect for runners who want evidence-based answers to training and nutrition questions.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/science-of-ultra/id1042673386) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/0N7NhFlgPuNsOvXZBLCpb0) &middot; [Website](https://www.scienceofultra.com/)

## News & Commentary

### The Runner's World Podcast
**Host:** Runner's World Editors &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 35 min

The official podcast from Runner's World magazine featuring gear reviews, training tips, and interviews with the running community's most interesting people.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/the-runners-world-podcast/id1527752182) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/4PYzKpMqkJLBfJO9Pqy2Xe) &middot; [Website](https://www.runnersworld.com/podcast/)

### CITIUS MAG Podcast
**Host:** Chris Chavez &middot; **Frequency:** Multiple times weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 45 min

News and commentary on professional track and field and road racing from the team at CITIUS Magazine. Great for staying current on the elite running scene.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/citius-mag-podcast/id1208825033) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/1h8LT3FVKqkhZAJMhxbMBM) &middot; [Website](https://citiusmag.com/category/podcast/)

### Clean Sport Collective
**Host:** Various anti-doping advocates &middot; **Frequency:** Monthly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 45 min

Discussions about doping in sports, clean competition, and the science of drug testing. Important listening for understanding the integrity challenges in endurance sports.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/clean-sport-collective/id1544308099) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/7gTqRvAeQ2QXMC4z8cTjm8) &middot; [Website](https://www.cleansportcollective.com/podcast)

## Interviews & Stories

### Trail Runner Nation
**Host:** Scott Warr & Don Freeman &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 55 min

Long-running podcast dedicated to trail and ultra running with interviews, race coverage, and training discussions. A staple of the trail running community since 2011.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/trail-runner-nation/id457457210) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/6aSKU2OYpBFvmKhgPLcf8X) &middot; [Website](https://trailrunnernation.com/)

### Running Rogue
**Host:** Chris McClung &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 60 min

Austin-based running community podcast covering everything from elite racing to everyday running life. Mix of interviews, training talk, and running culture.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/running-rogue/id963066104) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/3a6V7xQDXhSRdFDO7sjR5l) &middot; [Website](https://runningrogue.com/)

### Extramilest Podcast
**Host:** Floris Gierman &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 65 min

European perspective on running with interviews featuring international athletes and coaches. Good for exposure to the global running scene beyond the US.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/extramilest-podcast/id1109314482) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/3sATw2S4uIXnlQCG8LjGwA) &middot; [Website](https://www.extramilest.com/podcast/)

## Inspiration & Lifestyle

### Some Work, All Play
**Host:** Nick Symmonds &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 50 min

Olympic runner Nick Symmonds and friends discuss running, sports business, and life with humor and insight. Great balance of entertainment and substance.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/some-work-all-play/id1215877304) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/3BmRGAzZHBJTqWh2bMn8Rb) &middot; [Website](https://someworkallplay.com/)

### Running for Real
**Host:** Tina Muir &middot; **Frequency:** Weekly &middot; **Avg Duration:** 55 min

Elite runner turned podcaster Tina Muir focuses on the mental side of running, featuring conversations about motivation, setbacks, and finding joy in the sport.

[Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/running-for-real/id1269807707) &middot; [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/2loxPcZkEHjH6WT2fTJkpq) &middot; [Website](https://tinamuir.com/podcast/)
